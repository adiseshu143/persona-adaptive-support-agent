# Refund Policy

## Overview
Refund requests are handled by the billing operations team.

## Supported Cases
- Duplicate charge investigation
- Incorrect subscription renewal
- Service disruption review based on policy
- Billing dispute documentation

## Important Notes
The AI support assistant may explain the policy but must not promise a refund outcome.

## Escalation Rule
All refund demands must be escalated to a human support representative for identity verification and billing review.
