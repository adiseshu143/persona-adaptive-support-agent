SUBSCRIPTION UPGRADE AND DOWNGRADE POLICY

1. Upgrading a Plan
Upgrades (e.g. Starter to Pro, Pro to Enterprise) take effect immediately
upon confirmation. You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of
the current billing cycle at the new plan's rate, calculated automatically
at checkout.

2. Downgrading a Plan
Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle, not
immediately. This avoids losing access to features you've already paid
for in the current cycle. No partial refund is issued for the unused
portion of the higher-tier plan.

3. Feature Access During Transition
When a downgrade is scheduled, you retain full access to your current
plan's features until the transition date shown in Billing > Subscription.
After transition, any data or configuration exceeding the lower plan's
limits (e.g. seat count, storage) is not deleted but becomes read-only
until brought within the new plan's limits or the plan is upgraded again.

4. Cancelling a Scheduled Downgrade
A scheduled downgrade can be cancelled anytime before the transition date
from Billing > Subscription > Cancel Scheduled Change.

5. Seat-Based Plans
Adding seats mid-cycle is billed as a prorated upgrade. Removing seats
takes effect at the next renewal, consistent with the downgrade policy
above — seats cannot be removed for an immediate partial refund.

6. Enterprise Custom Contracts
Enterprise customers with a signed order form follow the upgrade/downgrade
terms specified in that contract, which may differ from the self-service
policy above (for example, fixed-term commitments with no mid-term
downgrade option). Always check the signed order form first for
Enterprise accounts.

7. Switching Between Monthly and Annual Billing
Switching from monthly to annual billing is treated as an upgrade and
takes effect immediately with proration. Switching from annual to monthly
takes effect at the next renewal date.
