Subscription Upgrade and Downgrade Guide

Customers may change their subscription plan from the billing settings page.

Upgrade Process
1. Open Billing Settings.
2. Select Change Plan.
3. Choose a higher plan.
4. Review prorated billing details.
5. Confirm the change.

Downgrade Process
1. Open Billing Settings.
2. Select Change Plan.
3. Choose a lower plan.
4. Review feature access changes.
5. Confirm the change.

Notes
Downgrades may apply at the next billing cycle depending on the subscription policy.
