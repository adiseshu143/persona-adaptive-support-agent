# Service Outage Response Guide

## Purpose
This guide explains how support should communicate during a service disruption.

## Response Principles
- Acknowledge the issue clearly
- Avoid speculation
- Share known impact only
- Provide the next update timeline if available

## Business Communication Guidance
For business stakeholders, focus on operational impact, affected services, and expected update timing.

## Escalation
If there is no documented workaround or if the outage affects critical operations, route the issue to a human incident response representative.
