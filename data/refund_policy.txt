REFUND POLICY

1. Eligibility Window
New subscriptions are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of the
initial purchase, no reason required. This applies to first-time
purchases only, not renewals.

2. Renewal Refunds
Refund requests for an annual renewal must be submitted within 7 days of
the renewal charge. Requests made after 7 days are evaluated case-by-case
based on actual product usage during the renewal period.

3. Partial Usage
If significant usage occurred during the eligibility window (for example,
heavy API consumption or data export on an annual plan), a prorated
refund may be issued instead of a full refund, at our discretion.

4. Non-Refundable Items
- Add-on credits that have already been consumed
- Custom onboarding or professional services fees
- Enterprise contracts with negotiated non-refundable terms specified in
  the signed order form

5. How to Request a Refund
Refund requests must be submitted through Billing > Request Refund, or by
contacting support with the invoice number and reason. Refund requests are
never processed instantly by an automated agent — every refund request
requires manual review by the billing team to confirm eligibility and
prevent fraud, even when the request appears straightforward.

6. Processing Time
Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-10
business days, depending on the card issuer or bank.

7. Disputed or Duplicate Charges
If you believe you were charged twice for the same billing period, this is
treated as a billing dispute rather than a standard refund request, and
must be escalated with both transaction IDs for the finance team to
reconcile against the payment processor.

8. Downgrades vs. Refunds
Switching to a lower-tier plan is a downgrade, not a refund. Downgrades
take effect at the next billing cycle with no partial-period refund for
the difference (see Billing Policy, Section 5).
